Hey everyone, my name's Eidref Navarro. You'll find me in the heart of Iloilo, specifically in the vibrant town of Estancia. But when the sun sets, I transform into something else entirely. My true passion lies in music, and I'm a DJ at heart.

There's nothing quite like the rush of spinning tunes and seeing the crowd lose themselves in the rhythm. The energy is palpable, and it's a feeling that's impossible to describe. I've been DJing for a while now, and I've learned that it's not just about playing the hottest tracks. I've been lucky enough to spin at a few Battle of the Sounds events near our area, and those are always a blast. The energy is electric, and it's amazing to see the different styles and techniques that other DJs bring to the table. It's a constant learning experience, and I'm always pushing myself to improve my skills and find new ways to surprise and delight the crowd. Music is my passion, and I can't wait to see where my journey takes me. Maybe one day, I'll be spinning at a massive festival, or maybe I'll just keep rocking the local scene. Whatever the future holds, I know that music will always be my driving force.
